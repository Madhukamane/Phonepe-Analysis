# 📊 PhonePe Business Insights Dashboard | Power BI Project

🚀 End-to-End Data Analysis Project using Power BI | Digital Payments, Loans & Financial Services Analytics

---

## 🧠 Problem Statement

With the rapid growth of digital payments in India, platforms like PhonePe handle millions of transactions across multiple services such as **Insurance, Loans, Money Transfer, and Recharge & Bills**.

However, businesses face challenges in:

* Identifying **high-performing services**
* Understanding **transaction success vs failure patterns**
* Tracking **monthly growth trends**
* Detecting **failure reasons impacting user experience**

👉 This project aims to analyze transaction data and provide **actionable insights to improve performance and reduce failures**.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze overall transaction volume and value
* Compare performance across different services
* Identify failure trends and reasons
* Track monthly transaction growth
* Provide business recommendations based on insights

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* **Power BI** – Dashboard & Data Visualization
* **Power Query** – Data Cleaning & Transformation
* **DAX** – KPI Measures & Calculations

---

## 📊 Dashboard Overview

The project consists of **5 interactive pages**:

### 1️⃣ Executive Summary

* Total Amount: **3,474M**
* Total Transactions: **300K**
* Successful Transactions: **288K**
* Failed Transactions: **10K**

✔ High success rate (~96%)
✔ Quick overview of platform performance

---

### 2️⃣ Insurance Analysis

* Balanced contribution across insurance types
* Monthly trend shows moderate fluctuations
* Majority transactions are successful

---

### 3️⃣ Loans Analysis

* 💰 **Loans contribute the highest revenue (~2,533M)**
* Auto Loan & Mutual Loan dominate
* Consistent monthly performance with peaks in mid-year

👉 Loans are the **primary revenue driver** of the platform

---

### 4️⃣ Money Transfer Analysis

* Total Amount: **378M**
* Success Rate: High (~96%)
* Failure Reasons:

  * Wrong PIN
  * Server Errors
  * Insufficient Balance

👉 Failure rate is slightly higher compared to other services

---

### 5️⃣ Recharge & Bills Analysis

* Total Amount: **51M**
* Most usage in:

  * Electricity
  * Mobile Recharge
  * DTH & Cable

✔ Stable and consistent monthly usage

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* 🚀 **Loans generate the highest revenue** among all services
* 📊 Overall **transaction success rate is very high (~96%)**
* ⚠️ Failures are mainly due to:

  * Wrong PIN
  * Server issues
  * Insufficient balance
* 📉 Some services show **seasonal fluctuations in monthly trends**
* 💡 Money Transfer has relatively higher failure contribution

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* 🔐 Improve user interface to reduce **Wrong PIN errors**
* ⚙️ Optimize backend systems to minimize **server failures**
* 🎯 Focus marketing efforts on **Loans (high revenue segment)**
* 📊 Monitor seasonal trends to plan campaigns effectively
* 🚀 Enhance reliability in Money Transfer services
